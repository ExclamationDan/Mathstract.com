Linear Equations

Vocabulary

Equation - A statement where two expressions are equal.

Solution/Root - The value(s) that make an equation true.

Back substitution - Plugging a computed solution into the origional equation.

Conditional equation - An equation that is true for only some values, but not all.

Identitity - An equation that is always true, such as 1 = 1.

Contradiction - An equation that is never true, such as 0 = 5.

Formula - An equation that models a relationship between two or more quantities.

Literal equation - An equation with at least two variables.

Object variable - The variable that is being solved.
